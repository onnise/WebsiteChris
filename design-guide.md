# Design Style Guide - Teak International

## Color Palette
- **Primary Color**: #8D6E63 (Warm Wood Tone)
- **Secondary Color**: #3E2723 (Dark Wood Tone)
- **Accent Color**: #D7CCC8 (Light Beige)
- **Text Color**: #212121 (Dark Charcoal)
- **Background**: #FAFAFA (Off-White)

## Typography
- **Headings**: 'Playfair Display', serif
  - Elegant, classic, used for section titles and hero text.
- **Body Text**: 'Montserrat', sans-serif
  - Clean, modern, legible, used for all body content and UI elements.

## Components
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Primary color background, white text.
  - Secondary/Ghost: Transparent background, white border (Hero).
- **Cards**:
  - Clean white cards with subtle shadow (`0 5px 15px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`).
  - Hover effect: Lift up (`translateY(-5px)`) and increased shadow.

## Spacing
- **Container Width**: 1200px
- **Section Padding**: 4rem (Desktop), 2rem (Mobile)
